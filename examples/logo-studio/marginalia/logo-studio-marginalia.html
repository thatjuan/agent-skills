Marginalia — Concept Review

April 15 2026 · Round 01 · No. 047 · 9 concepts

These nine concepts interpret the Marginalia brief across three strategic directions. Direction A holds the canonical line — the display-serif wordmark with the lowercase i drawn as a margin rule. Direction B makes the annotation system structural — highlighters and pen-strokes and a dog-eared page. Direction C takes the editorial form to its limit — a full broadsheet nameplate, the editor's own handwriting, and a library stamp masquerading as an icon. Each concept is shown in full colour, monochrome, and reversed.
